Common Questions

Why is there a free version?

We believe everyone should have access to these stories, regardless of their financial situation. If money or location is stopping you from getting this book, that shouldn't be a barrier. The message is more important than the profit.

Are the stories real?

Yes, every single story in this book is real. These are actual people who turned to Tahajjud during their darkest moments and witnessed transformations. Names and some details have been changed for privacy, but the experiences are 100% authentic.

What's the difference between digital and physical?

The digital edition is a PDF you download instantly after purchase. The physical edition is a professionally printed paperback available on Amazon with Prime shipping. Both contain the exact same content — the same stories, chapters, and practical guidance.

I love what you're doing. How can I support you?

JazakAllah khair! The best way to support is by leaving an honest review on Amazon and sharing the book with others who might benefit. If you'd like to contribute financially to help us keep offering free copies, you can support our work here.

Can I share the free PDF with others?

Yes! Share the link to PowerOfTahajjud.com/free so they can get their own copy.

Is this book only for Muslims?

While the book is rooted in Islamic teachings about Tahajjud (night prayer), the themes of hope, perseverance, and faith are universal. Anyone curious about spirituality, prayer, or finding strength during difficult times may find value in these stories.
